About Free Time Coffee

Free Time Coffee is India's premier café destination, celebrating the rich heritage of Indian coffee culture with a modern twist. Since 2010, we've been serving authentic Indian coffee blends sourced directly from the hills of Coorg, alongside traditional Indian snacks and delicacies.

Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming space where families, friends, and professionals can gather to enjoy exceptional coffee, delightful food, and meaningful conversations. Every cup of coffee we serve tells a story of our commitment to quality and tradition.
